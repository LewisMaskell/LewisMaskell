Tamper-Proof seal animation

3DS Max

Project Summary

This animation is freelance work I completed for a UK-Based industrial design company after finishing my 3d modelling University module.
I created this animation to demonstrate a locking lid and film seal for a coffee bean dispenser. This work was in collaboration with a 3d artist at the same company, that provided the static models to me.
This was my first industrial application of skills that I had picked up on my course, providing me valuable experience, working in a professional environment.

I have continued to work with this company on other projects since.
